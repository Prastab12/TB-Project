Overview
This dashboard monitors Tuberculosis (TB) performance indicators for Kathmandu District, Nepal across 5 Bikram Sambat fiscal years (BS 2078–2082, approx. 2021–2025). It visualises 13 NTP indicators — monthly case notification rates and annual treatment outcomes — derived from 780 FHIR R4 MeasureReport resources generated from DHIS2 source data. Use the Year filter to drill into a specific fiscal year, or Indicator to explore individual measures.
